Technology Innovations

Evaluation of Blue and Green Light Colors in 'Carabao' Mango (Hemiptera: Cicadellidae)

The study evaluates the blue and green light colors in the attraction of the leafhopper, Idioscopus clypealis, in 'Carabao' mango. The results showed that the blue light color is more attractive to the leafhopper compared to the green light color.

Design and Development of a Distance Monitoring Device: An OFW Application

The technology addresses a method for automatically monitoring the distance of an OFW from their home country. It uses a GPS module to track the location and a mobile application to display the distance.

Design and Development of Water Level Monitoring System for the Province of Zambales

The technology was developed as a monitoring tool by the local government of Zambales to prevent flooding. It uses sensors to measure water levels in rivers and streams and sends alerts to the local government units.
